Fly-Maybe

Passengers and frequent flyers are often caught unprepared by their flights’ delays, and that can often ruin a business trip or the start of a relaxing holiday. By looking at historical data from the 50 top USA airports, and the help of machine learning, we can make predictions about of possible delays our impending flight and be prepared!

By studying the flight’s data from last year and enriching it with very detailed weather data from the NOAA stations, we have learned which information allow us to make reliable predictions about the delay of an upcoming flight.

The dataset used contains information about all aspects of a particular flight; carrier, date, origin and destination, time of departure and arrival, statistics about taxing time, weather at the airports, and much more. All of this is leveraged by an algorithm to predict if your flight, within the next five days, will be delayed.

Have a look at the data used to train the algorithm, by looking at the recorded delays across airports in the USA in 2016, or just input the information about your flight and see if you should expect a smooth journey or not.
